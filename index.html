About This Events

International Conference on Innovative Computing, Informatics and Advanced Communication Systems for Sustainable Development

The aim and scope of the conference focus on new emerging trends in Information technologies with innovative computing approaches under Artificial Intelligence/machine learning, IoT, Big data technologies, Data science Robotic vision computing, Fuzzy approach, Autonomous computing, and Urban Computing. ICICIAC-2022 fosters basic and applied research that can improve people's quality of life. The primary goal of conference to bring together scientists, developers, and practitioners working in the field of advanced computing from academia and industries.

Also to encourage the transfer of basic research into institutionalized and industrialized research, as well as the conversion of applied investigation into real-time applications. Beside, this conference will creates opportunities for collaborating at national and international level and networking among Indian and international institutes to promote research

All accepted papers will be indexed in Scopus.

About Institue

Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) is established in 1997 by Dr. G Gangaraju as a self-financed institute under the aegis of Gokaraju Rangaraju Educational Society. GRIET is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, permanently affiliated to and autonomous under JNTUH, Hyderabad. GRIET is committed to quality education and is known for its innovative teaching practices.

Vision

To be among the best of the institutions for engineers and technologists with attitudes, skills and knowledge and to become an epicentre of creative solutions.

Mission

To achieve and impart quality education with an emphasis on practical skills and social relevance.

Quality Policy

To provide an integrated learning environment to enable students to grow towards their full potential and meet the high expectations of the Industry and the Society.

About the Department

The department of ECE started in the year 1997. The department is accredited by NBA and NAAC. The department of ers UG and PG courses, VLSI and Embedded Systems as specialization. The department has expertise adequate teaching faculty with industrial experience.
